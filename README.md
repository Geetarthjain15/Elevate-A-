# Elevate AI

Elevate AI is an advanced, AI-driven interview simulator designed to help candidates prepare for real-world job interviews across a wide variety of professional domains. By creating realistic mock interview experiences and providing instant, actionable feedback, Elevate AI empowers users to improve their technical knowledge, communication skills, and problem-solving abilities.

## Key Features

- **Multi-Domain Support**: Practice for 15 different professional roles, including Software Engineering (SDE 1 & 2), Data Analysis, Product Management, Marketing, Sales, HR, and more.
- **Customizable Interviews**: Tailor your mock interviews by selecting your desired domain, experience level (Junior to Lead), and the number of questions.
- **Voice-Powered AI Interviewer**: Engage in natural, spoken conversations with an AI interviewer powered by Vapi and Google Gemini, simulating the pressure and flow of a real interview.
- **Instant & Detailed Feedback**: Receive a comprehensive evaluation immediately after your interview, including an overall score and a breakdown of strengths and areas for improvement across:
  - Communication Skills
  - Technical Knowledge
  - Problem-Solving
  - Cultural & Role Fit
  - Confidence & Clarity
- **Dashboard & History**: Track your progress over time, review past interview transcripts, and revisit feedback to continuously refine your skills.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend & Backend**: Next.js (React)
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Authentication & Database**: Firebase
- **AI Voice Agent**: Vapi
- **Generative AI**: Google Gemini (via AI SDK)

## Getting Started

1. Clone the repository.
2. Install dependencies using `npm install`.
3. Set up your `.env.local` file with the required Firebase, Vapi, and Google Gemini API keys.
4. Run the development server using `npm run dev`.
5. Open `http://localhost:3000` to start practicing!
